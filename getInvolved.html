FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

How may I go about acquiring a Smoob?

It is illegal in most countries to own a Smoob Shark due to how difficult it is to keep them in good health, but we have several headquarters where we keep our Smoobs for research that you can visit.

Where are Smoobs found in the wild?

Smoobs are found worldwide, but typically can be found in just about any body of water despite their rarity. You may even find one at your local Burger King.
